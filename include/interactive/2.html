The Area

These are the areas of Lofoten, Vesterålen and Senja - Lovese in short. [More text about the area]

Lovese Drilling Zones

The body of water off the coast from Lofoten, Vesterålen and Senja is the epicenter of this story. For more than 50 years, these sea areas has remained untouched by the oil- and gas industry.
But this year, that could all change.

A big oil producer

If we zoom out, we see that Norway has opened vast sea areas for oil and gas exploration. The sea areas outside of Lovese are some of the last areas which has been spared.

A slim continental shelf

In this part of Norway, the continental shelf is at its most narrowest - creating some very favourable conditions for aquatic life.
