LOVESE

These are the areas of Lofoten, Vesterålen and Senja - Lovese in short.

The Norwegian Continental Shelf

In this part of Norway, the continental shelf is at its most narrowest - creating some very favourable conditions for aquatic life.

Breeding grounds

One of the most apparent effects is the breeding grounds. More than 80 percent of the North-Atlantic cod stock spends it first living years here.
