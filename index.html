LoVeSe Drilling



Across the world, local communities are fighting to stop new coal, oil and gas projects.

This interactive documentary explores how one of the most vulnerable and productive ecosystems in the world could be handed over to the oil industry in 2017.

The documentary is best experienced on larger screens. We suggest using landscape mode or a larger device for the best experience.
